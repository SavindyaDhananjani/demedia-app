What is DeMedia?

DeMedia is a decentralized social media protocol that empowers users by allowing them to store their personal information on their own devices, providing greater control over their data and content.

Why DeMedia?

DeMedia empowers users by allowing them to store their personal information on their own devices, providing greater control over their data and content. This helps users protect their privacy and strengthen their security by avoiding the drawbacks of centralized data storage.

How do DeMedia work?

DeMedia works by distributing control and ownership among users rather than relying on a single entity. This means that users have direct control over their data and content, which is stored on a distributed network of computers.

Do I need a my own peer to join to DeMedia?

Yes, You will need to set up your own peer on your device to join DeMedia.
